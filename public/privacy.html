Privacy Policy for Ruse

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Ruse ("the app") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage data from its users.

Specifically:

  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device data.
  • All gameplay happens locally on your device and is not sent anywhere.

If this changes in a future version of the app (for example, adding anonymous usage statistics), this policy will be updated to describe exactly what is collected and why, before that version is released.

Questions about this policy can be sent to lakicar.dev@gmail.com.
